Kitschy Craft Fun



Project: Crafts!
Time: 30 min to 2 hours
Supplies:
• Sunprints
• Blank paper, white or colored
• Ruler
• Scissors
• Glue
• Flat magnets
• Packing tape

These are some great projects for playing with all those old sunprints that you love but haven’t had a use for, or designing a new customized print for the occasion. Cards can be given for so many reasons, and it’s fun to add the extra touch by making it yourself. I came up with three card designs, using sunprints from past projects.



The bookmarks I made by laminating the front and back of the print with packing tape.



For the magnets, I found a few old ones and pasted my sunprints over the pictures—you can also get blank magnets at most craft stores. Have fun!
